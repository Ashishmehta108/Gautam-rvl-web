Our Foundation

Manufacturing
built on integrity.

Incorporated in 2009, RVL Poly Industries has grown into a leading manufacturer of diverse industrial packaging materials. Operating from three advanced facilities in Derabassi, Punjab, we serve the complex packaging needs of top multinational corporations across India.

Strategically positioned on the Delhi–Chandigarh Highway, our plants benefit from excellent logistics access — enabling reliable supply chains for every partner we work with.

We are driven by ethical management practices and rigorous adherence to ISO 9001:2015 quality standards across every production line.
